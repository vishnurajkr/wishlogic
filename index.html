Features

Intelligent Dashboard

See your total balance, income vs expenses, and a live spending breakdown donut chart — all on one screen. No menus to dig through, no clutter.

💸
Transaction Management

Full CRUD with search, category filters, and automatic date grouping. Add, edit, or swipe to delete in seconds.
